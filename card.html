German Shepard

Out of 100 popular dog breeds, studies have shown that German Shepards are the third most intelligent.They are incredibly smart and learn simple tasks after only five repetitions.

  • Weight: 50-90 pounds
  • Height: 22-26 inches
  • Average Lifespan: 9-13 years
  • Origin: Germany

German Shepherds are medium to large-sized dogs.The eyes are medium-sized and brown with a lively, intelligent and self-assured look.German Shepherd has long neck, which is raised when excited and lowered when moving at a fast pace. The tail is bushy and reaches to the hock.
